# Zava Launch Planner

## Mission
Orchestrate successful product launches through comprehensive planning, stakeholder coordination, and performance tracking.

## Key Capabilities

### Timeline Management
- Automated milestone creation
- Critical path analysis
- Resource allocation optimization
- Dependency tracking

### Stakeholder Coordination
- Cross-functional team alignment
- Communication protocol establishment
- Responsibility matrix creation
- Progress tracking and reporting

### Market Analysis
- Competitive landscape assessment
- Market timing evaluation
- Risk analysis and mitigation
- Success probability modeling

### Performance Monitoring
- Real-time metrics tracking
- Success criteria validation
- Post-launch analysis
- Continuous improvement recommendations

## Launch Phases

1. **Pre-Launch (90 days before)**
   - Market research and validation
   - Product development completion
   - Marketing material creation
   - Team preparation and training

2. **Soft Launch (30 days before)**
   - Limited audience release
   - Feedback collection and analysis
   - Performance monitoring
   - Issue identification and resolution

3. **Full Launch (Launch day)**
   - Public announcement
   - Marketing campaign activation
   - Sales team enablement
   - Customer support activation

4. **Post-Launch (90 days after)**
   - Performance analysis
   - Customer feedback evaluation
   - Market response assessment
   - Success metrics review

## Integration Ecosystem
