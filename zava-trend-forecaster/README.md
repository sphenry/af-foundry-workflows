# Zava Trend Forecaster

## Overview
Advanced AI-powered trend analysis and market prediction system that identifies emerging opportunities and potential disruptions across industries.

## Core Capabilities

### Trend Detection
- Multi-source data aggregation
- Pattern recognition algorithms
- Weak signal identification
- Trend lifecycle mapping

### Market Prediction
- Quantitative forecasting models
- Scenario planning and simulation
- Risk assessment frameworks
- Confidence interval analysis

### Opportunity Intelligence
- Market sizing and growth projections
- Competitive landscape analysis
- Investment opportunity scoring
- Strategic recommendation engine

### Real-time Monitoring
- Continuous signal tracking
- Automated alert systems
- Momentum analysis
- Sentiment monitoring

## Data Sources

### Primary Sources
- Social media platforms (Twitter, LinkedIn, Reddit)
- News and media outlets (Reuters, Bloomberg, TechCrunch)
- Industry publications and reports
- Patent filings and research papers

### Secondary Sources
- Economic indicators and market data
- Consumer behavior analytics
- Search trend analysis
- Investment and funding data

### Proprietary Sources
- Customer feedback and surveys
- Internal sales and usage data
- Partner ecosystem insights
- Expert network contributions

## Analysis Framework

### Trend Classification
- **Emerging**: Early stage, high uncertainty
- **Growing**: Gaining momentum, medium uncertainty
- **Mainstream**: Widely adopted, low uncertainty
- **Declining**: Losing relevance, repositioning needed

### Impact Assessment
- **Market Impact**: Revenue potential and market size
- **Operational Impact**: Process and workflow changes
- **Competitive Impact**: Advantage and threat analysis
- **Technology Impact**: Infrastructure and capability requirements
